The Gardens

Our Philosophy

Gardens as Living Art

The grounds of Lumina Villas are not manicured performances — they are cultivated ecosystems. Every tree, herb, and stone path has been placed in dialogue with the land's natural character.


We work with the seasons, not against them. Our team of eight full-time botanists and landscape stewards care for over 12 hectares of living landscape, ensuring that each visit feels different from the last.

Explore by Zone

Garden Rooms & Zones

The estate is divided into seven distinct garden zones, each with its own character, microclimate, and purpose.

Zone 1

The Lavender Corridor

Four hundred metres of hand-planted lavender framing the main approach to the estate.

Zone 2

The Ancient Olive Grove

500+ trees, some over 400 years old. Harvest each October for estate olive oil production.

Zone 3

The Spring Water Garden

Natural limestone channels carry spring water through a series of terraced reflection pools.

Zone 4

The Wildflower Meadow

An unharvested meadow zone that supports migratory pollinators and native wildlife.

Curated Experiences

What to Do in the Gardens

🌄

Dawn Herb Walks

Join our resident botanist for a 90-minute dawn walk through the herb garden and lavender fields, guided by the rising light and morning birdsong.

🫒

Olive Oil Tasting

Sample the estate's own cold-pressed extra-virgin olive oils in the stone oil house, guided by our sommelier.

🌿

Lavender Distillation

In summer, join the lavender harvest and watch traditional steam distillation produce the estate's signature essential oil.

🦋

Biodiversity Tour

A guided tour of the wildflower meadow and migratory bird habitat zones — a truly rare ecological experience.
